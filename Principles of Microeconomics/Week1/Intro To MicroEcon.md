[[Economics]] is the study of have community manages their scarce resources.

# Unlimited wants vs Limited Goods

[[Economic activity]]: how much buying and selling goes on in an economy over time
[[Economy]] : all the production and exchange activities that take place

## [[3 Economic Factors of Production]]
Land  - all the natural resources of the earth,
Labour - human effort
Capital  - equipment and structures used to produce the goods and service

# Opportunity Costs and utility

[[ Opportunity cost ]] - the price of the next best choice you give up when making a decision

[[ Benefits ]] - amount of satisfaction from an action.
[[ Costs ]] - price of an action

[[ Marginal utility ]] -  is the additional satisfaction or benefit a consumer gains from consuming one more unit of a good or service.

When faced with a choice of 2 products a rational person will continue buying a single product until a marginal utility per dollar of it is higher than marginal utility per dollar of the other.

# Trade
Trade Allows people to specialise, increases the variety and lowers costs for consumer.

# Type of Costs
[[ Explicit Costs ]]  - monetary payment made to a producer to acquire an item
[[ Implicit Costs ]] - opportunity cost of this choice

Total costs = Explicit costs + Implicit Costs

[[Sunk cost]] is a cost that has been incurred and cannot be reversed. A sunk cost exists regardless of choice. They are not a part of opportunity cost

# Definitions
[[ Economic growth ]]  - the increase in the amount of goods and services in an economy over a period of time.
[[ GDP per capita ]] -  market value of all final goods and services produced within a country over the population
