## ğŸŒ Comparative Economic Systems

### Traditional Economy
- **Basis**: Customs, traditions, barter
- **Traits**: Low innovation, high poverty, strong gender roles

### Command Economy
- **Basis**: Government control of production/distribution
- **Traits**: No private ownership, centralized decisions

### Free Market Economy
- **Basis**: Voluntary exchange, supply & demand
- **Traits**: Innovation, competition, minimal government

### Mixed Economy
- **Basis**: Blend of market freedom and government control
- **Traits**: Most modern economies; balance varies by country
