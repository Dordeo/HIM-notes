# Competition in Ultra Luxury Hotels
## What is ultra-luxury
1000$ per night and beyond. Incredible expensive and also incredibly demanding business from the side of the hotel.

It is the **fastest** growing industry segment in hospitality

Leader Growth region is **Asia Pacific** (Mostly India and China).

Target audience are younger people.

## Power of Scarcity
Rare and Limited

#### Economic Scarcity
When something is limited in supply and is rare, its value increases.

#### Psychological scarcity
When something feels are and exclusive, the desire for it grows.

Psychological scarcity drives the demand up, while economic scarcity

#### What is Perceived Scarcity

Scarcity is perceived differently by people from different cultures and different upbringing.


Scarcity is created from Experience, space and mostly access.

In USA for example it is shouting golden colours, while in UK it is subtle elegance. 

In China it is about tradition and access and in Japan it is minimalistic accuracy, seasonality, and detail on furniture and other things.

In India it is about Spirituality.
## CRM Tools

### Ultra Luxury Data Use
Collection of the data is very subtle.
The customer is being watched

Affinity program - no points, they are hidden from the customer

NO WORD DISCOUNT in the price tag

Anticipation before the arrival, constant feedback collection.

### Predictive Analysis
Observe(customer preferences collected quietly) - analyse - predict - act

### Guest Persona
Guest Persona - information about the guest that is turned into a virtual guest that follows most common behaviours.

Basically if you treat Guests as Pokemon, Guest Persona would be a Type.

Few Personas:
- Discovery traveller
- Returning guest
- Peace Seeker( person who wants quiet )
- Milestone celebrator

AI in CRMs to provide personalized experience for each guest.
