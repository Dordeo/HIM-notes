---
Word: To eat  
Characters: 吃饭  
Pinyin: chī fàn  
Tones: First and Fourth  
Meaning: To eat / To have a meal  
Category: Actions and Daily Life  
Usage: Used to describe the act of eating, especially meals.  
Example: 我想吃饭。  
Pinyin (Example): Wǒ xiǎng chī fàn.  
Translation: "I want to eat."
