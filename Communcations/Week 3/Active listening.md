[[ Communication Barrier ]] - anything that prevents you from receiving and understanding the messages others use to convey information, ideas and thoughts
# Common barrier in communication 
## Physical and environmental barriers
- noise
- time
- distance
- Choice of channel
-  health problem

## Language and Semantic barriers
- language, vocabulary
- [[Connotation]]  - various social overtones, cultural implications, or emotional meanings associated with a sign
- [[Denotation]] - explicit or referential meaning of a sign
-  accent

## Emotional / Psychological barriers
-  Emotions
-  Perception

## Cross - cultural barriers
- behavior
- emotions
- perceptions


# Active Listening
[[Communcations/Week 3/Active listening]] - conscious effort to pay undivided attention to what someone is saying


## 5 Steps to become an Active Listener
1) Pay attention
2) Show that you are listening - nodding, smiling
3) Provide Feedback - ask questions, reflect, summarize
4) Defer Judgement - allow speaker to finish
5) Respond - show respect. honesty
