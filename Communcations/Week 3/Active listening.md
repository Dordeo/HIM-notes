[[ Communication Barrier ]] - anything that prevents you from receiving and understanding the messages others use to convey information, ideas and thoughts
# Common barrier in communication 
## Physical and enviormental barriers
- noise
- time
- distance
- Choice of channel
-  health problem

## Language and Semantic barriers
- language, vocabulary
- [[Connotation]]  - various social overtones, cultural implications, or emotional meanings associated with a sign
- [[Denotation]] - explicit or referential meaning of a sign
-  accent

## Emotional / Psychological barriers
-  Emotions
-  Perception

## Cross - cultural barriers
- behaviour
- emotions
- perceptions


# Active Listening
[[Active listening]] - conscious effort to pay undivided attention to what someone is saying
