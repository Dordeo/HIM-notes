# The Objective

Types of communication :
- Verbal delivery 7%
- Vocal delivery 38%
- Visual delivery 55%

Verbal delivery  - choice of words
Vocal delivery  -  volume, rate of speech, pitch, grammar, pauses, Distracting vocal mannerism (filler words)
Visual delivery  -  posture, movement, appearance.
