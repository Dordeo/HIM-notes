# Business Meeting Essentials (2): Planning, Conducting & Managing Meetings

## Meeting Roles & Responsibilities

### The Leader
- Sets meeting time, location, and attendees
- Develops and guides the agenda
- Ensures equal participation
- Maintains a safe, positive atmosphere
- Assigns roles and action items
- Communicates conclusions and next steps

### The Recorder
- Helps develop and distribute the agenda
- Takes accurate notes (distinct from minutes)
- Transforms notes into formal meeting minutes
- Distributes minutes and updates
- Also fulfills participant duties

### The Timekeeper
- Manages time for each agenda item
- Oversees visual aids and equipment
- Coordinates with leader and recorder
- Also fulfills participant duties

### The Participant
- Understands agenda and meeting purpose
- Contributes ideas and collaborates
- Maintains respectful, safe atmosphere
- Speaks concisely and takes notes

## Team Dynamics & Power Types

### Team Composition
- Homogeneous Teams: Lower performance
- Diverse Teams with Inclusive Leaders: High performance
- Diverse Teams with Non-Inclusive Leaders: Low performance

### Types of Power
#### Power Over **oppression**
- Built on control, coercion, and fear

#### Power With **compassionand trust**
- Collaborative, respectful, and supportive

#### Power To **active influence**
- Focused on autonomy and potential

#### Power Within **self capacity**
- Awareness of self-worth and capacity
