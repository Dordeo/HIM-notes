# group Types

## Formal group
Designed and defined group with roles and purpose

## informal group
Unstructured, no commonalities but purpose

## command group
Chain of command, whk reports to whom. Hierarchy

## task group
Group of people working on three same  goal. Not interest, goal, structured.

## interest group
Informal task group.
