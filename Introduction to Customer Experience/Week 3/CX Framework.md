# Customer Experience Framework

## Most Important
The most important part of CX Framework is relations between customer and the company. Aka **trust**.

Key attributes 
• Show company has **integrity**, not just about making money
• **Trust** in companies declining around world. Why? 
• Show customers company acts in **their best interest**

- Make customers feel important, valued & cared for actively listening & asking right questions 
- Meet dead lines you give customers& follow up when you said you would 
- Keep language clear & simple, if jargon used, customers may think company hide something
- Staff must have expertise to solve customer queries-many customers will have done research themselves

# Emotional Drivers
