# Customer Expectations

[[Customer Expectations]] - ‘The standard of experience that a customer expects from a company’

Customers are used to innovation - expectations are rising fast. Amazing cutting edge experience today could be outdated tomorrow. Innovation / disruption impact what we expect from companies every day.

## Customer Promise Vs Expectations Gap
You do not want to promise more than you can handle, as it may lead to customer expectations

[[Customer Promise]] - what a customer is promised by the company.

[[Expectations Gap]] - difference between what the customer was promised and what the customer received.

The **smaller** the [[Expectations Gap]] is the **larger** will be the Trust gain in the **Emotional Piggy bank**.
