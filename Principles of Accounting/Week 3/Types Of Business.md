# Main types of business

 - Sole proprietorship  - A business owned and operated by one individual.
 - Partner ship -  business owned and run by two or more individuals, called partners, easy to set up.
 - Limited liability Company - A business owned and run by one more individuals, called members.
 - Corporation - 1 or more, called stockholders; can 
get many investors by selling stock or shares of corporate ownership.


# **IMPORTANT**
[[Assets]] = Liabilities - Equity
Assets are all things company owns.
Liabilities are debts
Equity is what shareholders own of the company
