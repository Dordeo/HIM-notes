# Assets
Type of Assets :
- [[Current Assets]] - all assets that can be converted to cash within a single year. Example being: Cash and currency, Account Receivable, short time investments, inventory(current items for sale).
- [[Non-current assets]] - all assets that cant be converted within a single year. Example being property and equipment, Long term investment and intangible assets.

## Account Receivable
[[Account Receivable]] - is a payment expected to be paid by the customer by a certain date. Example being subscription.

# Liabilities
Type of Liabilities :
- [[Current Liabilities]] - debts and other liabilities that are effective within one year.
- [[Non-current liabilities]] - liabilities that need to be settled after a year or more.

## Account Payable
[[Account Payable]] - money the company owes for a product it has bought, if it decides to pay at a later date.
