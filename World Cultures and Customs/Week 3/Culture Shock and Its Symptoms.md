# Culture Shock
[[Culture Shock]] - an extreme response to an international transition.

An awareness that one's basic assumptions about life and one's familiar ways of behaving are no longer appropriate or functional.
## Symptoms
#### Emotional Symptoms:
- Homesickness 
- Feeling of Isolation
- Irritability that comes from unexplained source
- Anxiety 
- Loss of confidence in self, doubt

#### Physical
- Disrupted sleep schedule
- Changes in appetite
- Frequent headache
- Susceptibility to minor illnesses

#### Behavioral
- Difficulty concentrating
- Withdrawal from social interactions
- Idealization of home culture
- Antagonization of host culture



## Stage Of Culture Shock
### Honeymoon Phase
Everything feels great. The happiness level of the person when they came from home to foreign place is actually higher. than before. Everything feels new and interesting, focus on broad than daily life

### Frustration or Culture Shock Phase
The person feels irritated and disoriented. The person feels overwhelmed by the new culture. They don`t understand, it is either caused by language barrier and homesickness.

### Adjustments Phase
Small shifts in the person perception of the host culture, the world around them. Frustration is still there but it is going away. More relationships with people.

### Adaptation Phase
Enjoyment phase. The person can actually thrive inside the new culture. Like fish back in water.

## Reactions

Culture Surprise -> Cultural Irritation -> Culture Stress -> Culture Fatigue -> Culture Shock
