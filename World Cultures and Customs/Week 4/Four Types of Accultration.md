# Acculturation 

[[Acculturation]] - second culture learning: adapting a culture that is not your own.

## Enculturation
[[Enculturation]] - first learning of the culture.

## Four Types of Acculturation
### Integration 
[[Integration]] - adopting some values from the new culture but the original cultural identity is still the same. It is more of multicultural personalization.

### Assimilation
[[Assimilation]] - becoming one with the culture. Complete adoption of the new culture.

### Separation
[[Separation]] - Rejection of the new culture. Staying with ones own culture.

### Marginalization
[[Marginalization]] - Rejection of both the new and the old cultures. creation of ones own culture through a mix of both.
