# Types of data

[[ Data ]]can be of two distinct types 
- [[ Qualitive data ]] - data in a form of words or descriptions
- [[ Quantitative data  ]] - numbers or measurements

# Ethics
- Consent of transparency  - the consumer must always know what data is collected and how.
- Minimization  - collect minimum, only what is essential not cause you want.
- Anonymization - remove personal identifiers before analysis.
- Access Control - limit who can view or export row data.
- Retention Policy - limit for how long the data is stored. 
### What to use of data
Quantitative data is good of describing questions like How much ? How often ?
Qualitive data is good at describing What? How? Why?

Quantitative helps to notice the problem,  if it even exists.
Qualitive helps to understand the cause of the problem.

# Other sub-types of data
## [[Sub-types of Quantitative]] 
- Discrete  - whole numbers
- Continuous  - numbers with decimals
- Ratio - numbers with true null/zero point
- Interval - numbers with equal spacing but no true zero
- Percentage - % numbers

## [[Sub-types of Qualitive  ]]
- Nominal  - unordered categories
- Ordinal  - ordered categories
- Binary data - 2 possible answers
- Observational  - insights captured by observing
