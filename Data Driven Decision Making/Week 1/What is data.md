## What is data
[[Data]] is any form of information that has been processed and can be used  to produce a sound result from logical reasoning.

## What is intuition or guessing
[[Intuition]] is a feeling.
Intuition can be based on beliefs or gut feeling or personal experience . 

Success of intuition is based on luck .

## What is a decision
[[Decision]] in an informed choice when faced with multiple choices and their potential outcomes. 
 A good decision is always :
 - Goal driven - made to achieve a certain result
 - Action-Oriented - consists of action /s
 - Fact-Based - The decision is supported by data and relavant analysis.
