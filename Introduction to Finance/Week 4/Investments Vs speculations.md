# Investment 
[[Investment]] - **long term** position, taken in account with multiple consideration and POVs. It is a purchase in the present for a stock or a share of a company with a purpose to sell it in a far future.
- Long Term
- Management of Patience
- Real economy

# Speculation
[[Speculation]] - **short term** position, kind of like a bet so that a stock purchase pays off in a very short term.
- Bet
- Short Term
- Financial Product


# Supply of Money
[[Physical Supply]] - physical money, printed by the government
[[M1 Money Supply]] - liquid money like checks, cash, deposits, credit cards and etc.
[[M2 Money Supply]] - M1 + Savings

There are more till M8

Practically [[M3-M4]] are moderately liquid. They are long term saving accounts, investments.
[[M5-M7]] - shadow banking and financial modeling that are expected to bring in money.

# Derivatives
[[Derivative]] - a contract between two or more groups that has its monetary value tied to performance of a certain product that derivative is based upon.
