# Credit
[[Revolving Credit]] - credit or debt that you can continually borrow, repay and reborrow. Basically fluid form of debt that can be taken whenever repaid after a certain date. 

## Loans with Fixed repayment

A loan that is to be repaid regularly based on a set period with a set or fixed amount [[Principles]] + [[Interest Rate]]


### Fixed rate vs Variable Rate

[[Fixed rate]] = The interest rate remains the same throughout the entire duration of the loan. 
Advantages: Predictable monthly payments, protection against rising rates. 
Disadvantage: May be higher than the variable rate at the beginning.


[[Variable rate]] = The interest rate changes according to a reference index (e.g., LIBOR, SARON).
Advantages: Often lower at the start, possibility to benefit from falling rates. 
Disadvantages: Uncertainty about future costs, risk of increasing monthly payments.
