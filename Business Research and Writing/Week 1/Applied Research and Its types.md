# What is applied research
[[Applied Research]] involves using theoretical knowledge to solve concrete, real concrete, real-world Problem. Unlike fundamental academic research, which seeks to expand knowledge for its own sake, applied research has a practical purpose and aims to address specific challenges faced by organizations, communities, or industries.

Applied research builds upon existing academic knowledge and transforms its foundation into solution.

## Methodology
1. Literature review - analyze existing studies
2. Best practice identification - Extract proven strategies from texts
3. Contextual adaptation - tailor these evidence - based on your problem
4. Implementation idea -  develop actionable recommendations that can be done
