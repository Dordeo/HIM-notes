# Types of Reservations
### Guaranteed Reservations 
- Pre- payment: Full payment in advance
- Advance deposit: Partial payment
[[ Guaranteed Reservations ]] are guaranteed as the revenue is guaranteed upon the booking by either a deposit on [[Online Travel Agency]], credit information or full payment in advance

[[ Non-guaranteed Reservations ]] - the opposite, revenue is not secured. It allows for guests to be more flexible and attracts more customers.

## Sources of Reservations
- Directly with the hotel
- [[CRO (Central Reservation Office)]]  - call office( ONLY CALL OFFICE)
- [[ Global Distribution Systems ]] 
- [[ Central Reservation System ]]- online reservations AND call office included
- Online Travel Agency
- [[ Internet Distribution Systems ]]

# Determining Room and Rate Availability
## Accept as Requested
If the room required is availble, Proceed next step

Else **suggest alternatives**,  **waitlist** , or **alternative property**.

## Overbooking
To ensure 100% occupancy. If all guests are coming you will have to walk the guest to the next hotel.
