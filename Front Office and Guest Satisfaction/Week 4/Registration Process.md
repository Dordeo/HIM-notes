# Registration Process

## Pre-registration Activity
- Room Assignments
- Registration forms
- Key Card Encoding
- Special Arrangements
## Creating Registration Record
#### Essential Guest Information
###### Personal Details
Full name, address, phone number and contact information

###### Business Information
Company affiliation and corporate account details 

###### Identification
Passport info

###### Stay Details
Arrival and departure dates, room type, special rate code

###### Payment methods
Guest signature and payment information

## Room and Rate Assignment
#### Room Assignment
- Based on guest preference
- Room availability
- Rate structure - preferred rate the guest is willing to pay
- Location Preference
- Future in sight
#### Rate assignment
- [[Rate]] - amount of money the guest pays per day:
	- 1) Standard - [[Rack Rate]] 100%
	- 2) Corporate below Rack Rate
	- 3) Special even less

## Payment Method Set up
Preparation for the check out, It is so that the amount of skippers (look [[PMS and Guest Profile]]) is reduced.

There are a few methods:
- Cash
- Credit
- Digital
- Direct Bill

## Room Key and Guest Orientation
When the **Room Key** is issued the registration process is usually **completed**

This step is basically guiding the guest to the room and giving them the keys.


## Managing Special Request
An opportunity to gain guest loyalty and upkeep it . Always documented in the guest profile if they are a special target.

## Revenue Enhancement 
#### Up-selling
When selling a room start with more expensive towards the least expensive.
