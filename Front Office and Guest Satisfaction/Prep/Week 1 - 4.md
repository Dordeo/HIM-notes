## üìã Reservations

### Types of Reservations
- **Guaranteed Reservations**: Revenue secured via pre-payment, credit card, or travel agent voucher. Held for entire night.
- **Non-Guaranteed Reservations**: Held until 6 p.m., no financial commitment, higher no-show risk.

### Sources of Reservations
- Direct booking with hotel
- CRO (Central Reservation Office): phone-based
- CRS (Central Reservation System): phone + online
- GDS (Global Distribution Systems)
- IDS (Internet Distribution Systems)
- OTA (Online Travel Agency)

### Room & Rate Availability
- **Accept as Requested**: Room and rate available
- **Suggest Alternatives**: Different room type, rate, or dates
- **Waitlist Option**: Hold spot in case of cancellation
- **Alternative Property**: Refer to sister or nearby hotels

### Confirmation Methods
- **Confirmation Number**: Unique ID for tracking
- **Confirmation Letter**: Formal document with booking details, cancellation policy

## üìà Revenue Management

### Definition
Strategic pricing and inventory control to maximize revenue. Also known as Yield Management.

### Revenue Management Criteria
- Perishable inventory
- Demand forecasting
- Profit focus

### Forecasting Techniques
- Room availability
- Occupancy projection
- Market segmentation
- Seasonal variation

### Revenue Management Cycle
1. **Forecast**: Analyze supply/demand
2. **Optimize**: Target markets and pricing
3. **Generate Revenue**: Apply strategies
4. **Monitor**: Track performance
5. **Control**: Adjust in real time

### Key Formulas
- **Occupancy Rate** = Rooms Sold √∑ Rooms Available
- **Average Daily Rate (ADR)** = Room Revenue √∑ Rooms Sold
- **Yield Percentage** = Actual Revenue √∑ Potential Revenue
- **RevPAR** = Room Revenue √∑ Rooms Available
- **PAR (Potential Average Rate)** = Potential Revenue √∑ Rooms Available
- **Actual Revenue** = **Room Revenue** = Rooms Sold * Rates at which rooms where sold
- **Potential Revenue** = Rooms Available * Rack Rate

### Implementation Strategies

#### High Demand Tactics
- Rate optimization
- Stay controls (minimum length)
- Inventory management (reduce group allocations)
- Advanced bookings with deposits

#### Low Demand Tactics
- Value positioning and packages
- Flexible policies and lower rates
- Revenue enhancement via upgrades
- Staff engagement in revenue generation
