# SWOT analysis
It is an analysis method that explore areas of the product such as
### Strength (Internal)
- qualities
- capabilities
- assets
- pros
### Weaknesses (Internal)
- cons
- lacks
- liabilities
- areas of improvement
### Opportunities (External)
- connections
- investment
- social tread
- expansion
### Threats (External)
- competition
- wars
- obstacles
- natural disasters
